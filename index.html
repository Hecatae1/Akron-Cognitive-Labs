01 — Our Story

Built on curiosity, shaped by care.

Akron Cognitive Labs began with a simple belief: the most useful tools are the ones that respect how people actually think. We study attention, memory, and decision-making — then turn what we learn into calm, practical software for the people and teams we serve.

Based in Akron, working with everyone. Small by design, careful by nature, and always listening more than we speak.
